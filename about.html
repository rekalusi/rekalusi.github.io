About

Rekalusi is a Malaysian based digital advertising company that was established in Jun 2017. The word "rekalusi" is taken from both Malay word "reka", meaning design and "iIusi" meaning illusion. When both words are combined we feel that it is actually close to another Malay word, "revolusi" meaning revolution. Hence our tagline, "Digital Revolution"



Digital Revolution

"Digital Revolution" literally reflects our vision in starting a digital revolution over the internet. We strongly believe in introducing economy in production, minimizing carbon footprints by providing applicable digital solutions and advertising support to businesses with the lowest offer to suit different budgets.



Why Us?

We promote economy in cost, simplicity in our design and directness in our advertising. Each marketing strategy will be set and customized according to client's business needs so the results that we produce will be individually unique. rekalusi.com also serves as a place where we share our experiences, ideas, philosophy and inspirations in everyday lifestyles

About Us

Located in Johor Bahru, Barelang is a non-profit-organization that was established in 2016. This means that income from sales is channeled directly to the cat shelters all around Johor Bahru area. In providing cat’s product and care-services to consumers primarily located in Johor and Singapore, Barelang offer various product for cat-owners & cat-lovers aswell.

Why Us?

"Because we are very passionate about the well being of our feline friends, we hope to be able to apply knowledge & awareness to catlovers by sharing the right advices on proper cat care ..."

Audio edits

"Getaran Jiwa" (Vibration of the Soul) is a song composed and recorded by the late Tan Sri P. Ramlee, with the lyrics by S. Sudarmaji. The song was also sung by his actor Ghazali, to Tengku Zaleha (Saadiah) in the film Antara Dua Darjat aired in 1960 "Getaran Jiwa" has achieved a "evergreen" rating so many other artists re-record this song in various forms, from now on.

We were playing around with a software called "cakewalk" and decided to edit some bass line and drums to this song. Enjoy!
